**Bubble duck** Bubble Duck is a chaotic local multiplayer game where you and your friends battle it out in a slippery bathtub. Push each other out, claim your space, and become the true master of the bubbles! Fast, funny, and guaranteed to cause laughter (and maybe a few friendly grudges)..

 This was the game made for the Global Game Jam 2025 within 48 hours!!

- **Genre:** 2D, local multiplayer, party game.  
- **Tools:** Unity, C#.
- **My Rol:** Main programmer, assets integrator.
